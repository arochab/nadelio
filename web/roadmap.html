Roadmap

What is shipped, and where this is heading.

Nadelio is a measurement instrument for AI visibility, built in the open. Here is what already works, what is landing next, and the direction, without the vaporware.

Shipped

Live now

Working today, on every audit, free.

  • Dual visibility mapGoogle SERP and AI answers measured side by side, so you see the gap between where you rank and where you get recommended.
  • Bounded GEO scoreEvery score reported with its confidence interval and an honest stable or volatile verdict, never a bare number.
  • Real lead detectionHead to head with your top rival, showing whether a lead is real or just noise once the intervals overlap.
  • Web verified identityWe read your real website before measuring, so an ambiguous name never audits the wrong company.
  • Market aware searchAudits run against the right country and language, not a US default.
  • Board ready Deep AuditA written verdict, a defensible score, a remediation plan, and a downloadable dossier you can forward.
  • Instant, no signupOne brand name, a full competitive read in about ten seconds, no account required.
